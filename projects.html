Robotics

During high school, I joined a robotics team in Emek Hafar.
Each year, the global organization FIRST presented a task that
required us to build a robot suitable for that specific mission.
After constructing the robot, there was a national competition
where we competed against robots from different teams.
The winning robot advanced to the finals and participated
in the global competition in St. Louis, USA.

Through robotics, I acquired various skills, including:

  • Exposure to programming
  • Teamwork
  • Knowledge acquisition
  • Responsibility
  • Mechanical skills
